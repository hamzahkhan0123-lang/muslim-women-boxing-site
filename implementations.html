Implementation Plan for Inclusive Boxing Programme

This plan outlines the steps to launch and sustain a boxing initiative for Muslim women, respecting cultural and religious values.

Evidence of Need

Sport England’s Active Lives survey (2023) shows that only 18% of Muslim women in England participate in sport weekly, compared to 36% of the general female population. Barriers include lack of culturally appropriate spaces, limited female coaches, and concerns about modesty. Local consultations with community leaders in Birmingham confirm strong interest in female-only boxing sessions as a safe and empowering activity.

Policy Context & Delivery Structures

This initiative aligns with Sport England’s Uniting the Movement strategy, which prioritises tackling inequalities in sport participation. The Equality Act 2010 requires providers to make reasonable adjustments for religious and cultural needs. Local delivery will involve partnerships with Birmingham City Council Sport Development Team and England Boxing, ensuring compliance with safeguarding standards and coach accreditation.

Sociological Analysis

The programme addresses intersectionality by recognising how gender, religion, and ethnicity combine to create unique barriers for Muslim women. Issues of social exclusion arise from limited access to culturally appropriate facilities, while cultural capital is enhanced through boxing as a confidence-building and empowering activity. These concepts inform the design of female-only sessions and culturally sensitive coaching.

Phase 1: Planning & Research

  • Engage with community leaders and potential participants
  • Identify suitable venues and coaches
  • Conduct needs assessment

Phase 2: Setup & Training

  • Purchase equipment
  • Train coaches in cultural sensitivity
  • Develop inclusive training schedules

Phase 3: Launch

  • Begin sessions with pilot group
  • Collect feedback and adjust program
  • Promote through local networks

Phase 4: Evaluation & Expansion

  • Evaluate outcomes and participant satisfaction
  • Seek additional funding
  • Expand to other communities

  • Female-only sessions and privacy screens
  • DBS checks for all coaches
  • Emergency procedures and first aid provision
  • Compliance with England Boxing safeguarding policy
