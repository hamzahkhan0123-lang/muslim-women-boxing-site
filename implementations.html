Implementation Plan for Inclusive Boxing Program

This plan outlines the steps to launch and sustain a boxing initiative for Muslim women, respecting cultural and religious values.

Phase 1: Planning & Research

  • Engage with community leaders and potential participants
  • Identify suitable venues and coaches
  • Conduct needs assessment

Phase 2: Setup & Training

  • Purchase equipment
  • Train coaches in cultural sensitivity
  • Develop inclusive training schedules

Phase 3: Launch

  • Begin sessions with pilot group
  • Collect feedback and adjust program
  • Promote through local networks

Phase 4: Evaluation & Expansion

  • Evaluate outcomes and participant satisfaction
  • Seek additional funding
  • Expand to other communities
