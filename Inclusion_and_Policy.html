UK Agencies and Policy Interaction: Creating Inclusive Boxing Pathways

Inclusive boxing development in the UK is shaped by a coordinated system of national agencies, community organisations, and local authorities. Together, they provide strategic leadership, funding, cultural expertise, and safeguarding guidance that enable clubs to create environments that are accessible and respectful for Muslim women and other underrepresented groups.
