Monitoring & Evaluation Framework

Monitoring and evaluation are essential to ensure this initiative achieves its intended outcomes and aligns with UK sport development standards. This framework connects directly to our logic model, measuring progress towards increased participation, improved wellbeing, and social inclusion. Indicators have been selected based on Sport England evaluation guidelines and sociological principles of empowerment and equity.

Sociological Rationale: Indicators such as retention and satisfaction address barriers to participation and give voice to underrepresented groups.

Indicator Method Frequency Outcome Link Feedback Loop
Attendance numbers Session registers Weekly Improved access and participation Programme coordinator reviews weekly and adjusts session times within 2 weeks
Participant satisfaction Anonymous surveys Quarterly Empowerment and positive experience Modify coaching approach based on survey results
Retention rate Compare enrolment vs. ongoing participation Every 6 months Sustained engagement and inclusion Inform expansion and resource allocation decisions
Health and wellbeing outcomes Self-reported physical activity and confidence Baseline + 6 months Improved physical and mental wellbeing Report to funders and stakeholders; adjust programme design
